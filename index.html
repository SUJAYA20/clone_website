    About Us



    Vision


    To emerge as a centre of excellence by producing technically sound and ethically strong engineers who will be adaptive, accommodative, innovative and cooperative by nature coupled with leadership quality in order to serve the industry as an effective resource, encouraging them to go for higher study and also motivating them to become successful entrepreneurs for generating employment to serve the society and the nation in ultimate.


    Mission


    M1. To create an environment conducive to the development of innovative ideas, entrepreneurship and human values along with the emergence of highly skilled engineers and future leaders.
    M2. To impart knowledge and training in modern, creative ways, compatible with industry and society at large, to address socio-economic challenges from local to national levels.
    M3. To collaborate with academic and scientific institutions all around the world to improve the academic and research environment in order to nurture creative minds and talents.

    Message From Principal


    Dr. Maloy Kumar Chandra Techno India-Banipur which started its journey in 2008 has now grown in to one of the premier institution within a decade. Today, with its sprawling college buildings, an eco-friendly environment surrounded by lush green meadows amidst a calm, serene and picturesque landscape, it provides an appropriate environment for academics and overall development of students’ skill. Today the college boasts of latest state-of-the art laboratories, well equipped workshops, an ever growing library and above all a strong contingent of highly efficient and dedicated faculty members who groom the students to become quality engineers in their respective fields in line with the ever changing industrial scenario, so as to produce a band of innovative and brilliant industry ready professionals. Our value based quality education and holistic training ensures that they are equipped to serve any corner of the world. Techno India Banipur has ever endeavored to impart to its students a scientific and broadly humanistic outlook in preparation for their life in today’s modern world. We hold that education is not summed up in mere learning, but implies building up the whole character and personality, fostering a sense of deep moral and religious convictions. Techno India Banipur has been especially successful in maintaining a friendly family spirit among staff and students. Techno India Banipur thus acts as a catalyst to fulfil the dreams of aspiring engineers in shaping their future.

       SWAYAM                                IIRS-ISRO


    TECB has gone beyond traditional classroom teaching to provide e-learning facilities to the students,using the latest technologies and associated themselves with the IIRS outreach programs. Under this program two modes of content delivery system has been developed using online learning platforms: Live and interactive mode (known as EDUSAT), E-learning mode In addition, NPTEL courses, on MOOC platform, organised by IIT Madras,which has launched thousands of courses relating to engineering,mathematics, computer science,physics and chemistry are taught by faculty members of IITs/NIT/IISc/IMM

    Institution's Innovation Council (IC201912096)

    Ministry of Human Resource Development (MHRD), Govt. of India has recently established ‘MHRD’s Innovation Cell (MIC)’ to systematically foster the culture of Innovation amongst all Higher Education Institutions (HEIs). Across the institutions, MIC established Institution’s Innovation Council (IICs) to promote a spirit of entrepreneurship and innovation among the elite institutions in the country through multitudinous modes leading to an innovation promotion eco-system in these campuses. The primary mandate of MIC is to encourage,inspire and nurture young students by supporting them to work with innovative ideas while they are in formative years.
    We are proud to inform you that among other institutions of prominence among the country, Techno Engineering College Banipur (TECB) has joined the elite class of country’s institutions to have established its own IIC under the Ministry of HRD’s Innovation Cell (MIC).
